Scrambled Eggs

Description

A dish made from beaten eggs, typically mixed with salt and sometimes milk or butter. The beaten eggs are stirred gently while cooking over low-to-medium heat.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk or cream (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack and whisk — beat the eggs together with a pinch of salt and a splash of milk or cream if using.
  2. Heat the pan — melt butter in a pan over low to medium-low heat.
  3. Add the eggs — pour in the egg mixture.
  4. Stir slowly — using a spatula, gently push and fold the eggs as they cook. Don't rush it.
  5. Pull off the heat early — remove the pan just before the eggs look fully done, as they'll continue cooking from residual heat.
  6. Season and serve — finish with black pepper and serve immediately.
